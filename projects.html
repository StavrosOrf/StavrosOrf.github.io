Projects

EV2Gym

A flexible simulation environment for EV smart charging research. It provides an OpenAI Gym interface for developing and benchmarking reinforcement learning solutions in the Vehicle-to-Grid domain.

DRIVE2X

The DriVe2X project aims to accelerate the uptake of vehicle electrification through new bi-directional smart charging technologies and widespread awareness of V2X, fostering their roll-out in European smart cities.
