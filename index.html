Stavros Orfanoudakis

Research Fellow · Power Systems Laboratory, ETH Zürich

I am a Research Fellow at the Power Systems Laboratory, ETH Zürich, where I work on reinforcement learning, graph neural networks, large language models, and physics-informed machine learning for power and energy systems, with a focus on building scalable and trustworthy AI methods for real-world energy-system operation.

Research interests
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Physics-Informed ML
  • LLMs & GNNs for Power Systems
  • Energy Transition & Smart Grids
  • Multi-Agent Systems
