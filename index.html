Stavros Orfanoudakis

Research Fellow · Power Systems Laboratory, ETH Zürich

I am a Research Fellow at the Power Systems Laboratory, ETH Zürich, where I apply the latest AI algorithms, including Reinforcement Learning (RL), large language models (LLMs), Graph Neural Networks (GNNs), and physics-informed machine learning to power and energy-system operation. During my PhD at TU Delft, I focused on scalable reinforcement learning for optimal electric-vehicle charging, advised by Dr. Pedro P. Vergara and Prof. Peter Palensky.

Before ETH, I was a visiting researcher at MIT's Center for Transportation and Logistics, where I worked on AI-based routing for electric truck fleets. I won the international PowerTAC competition in 2020 and placed second in 2021 as a student at the Technical University of Crete.
